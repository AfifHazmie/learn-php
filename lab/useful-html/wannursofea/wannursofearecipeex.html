Pesto Pasta

  • 1 lb. pasta
  • 1/2 cup pesto sauce
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup pine nuts
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  1. Cook pasta according to package instructions.
  2. In a separate pan, toast pine nuts until lightly browned.
  3. In a large bowl, mix cooked pasta, pesto sauce, Parmesan cheese, and toasted pine nuts.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
